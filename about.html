About

We built this firm to be the partner
we always wanted to have.

DalFort is a Dallas-based investment firm focused on the lower middle market. We partner with entrepreneurs and management teams in industrial manufacturing, specialty chemicals, building products, energy services, and logistics.

Our capital is permanent, sourced from family offices and institutions with long-term horizons. No fund timelines dictate our decisions. What drives us is building real, lasting value alongside the people who know their businesses best.
